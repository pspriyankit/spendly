Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — transaction amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and features used, to help us improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is a free tool and does not process any financial transactions on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues in the app.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate the platform (for example, hosting providers). These services are only given access to your data to the extent necessary to perform their functions, and are contractually obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We do not integrate with any external financial services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms that would receive your personal expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the data we hold about you, please reach out through the in-app support option.

We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently. You may also review our Terms and Conditions for more information about your rights and responsibilities when using Spendly.
